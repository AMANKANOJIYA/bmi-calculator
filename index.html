What is BMI?

BMI is the acronym for Body Mass Index, a parameter adopted by the World Health Organization for calculate the ideal weight of each person.

The index is calculated as follows: the patient's weight is divided by his height raised to the square. The individual is said to have normal weight when the BMI result is between 18.5 and 24.9.
